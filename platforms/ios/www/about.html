About
This app is part of Anaphylaxis Canada's Allergy Awareness Challenge. A program that helps high schools educating students about life threatening allergies in a fun way! It features three days of games and competitions promote allergy awareness and education as well as what students can do to keep their high school safe for everyone. Sign up for the program at www.allergychallenge.ca.
This app was developed for Anaphylaxis Canada. We are a national non-profit organization whose mission is to inform, support, educate and advocate for the needs of individuals and families with anaphylaxis and to support and participate in research.
For more information please visit www.anaphylaxis.ca.
